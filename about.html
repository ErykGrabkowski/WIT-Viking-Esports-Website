ABOUT US


We are WIT Viking Esports, a club that is a part of Waterford Institute of Technology. We take part in Irish Collegiate Esports and other local tournaments. Was it not for COVID we'd be having great craic playing some games casually in the college as well, but ah sure you know how it is. Keep in touch for updates on matches and events.

Why us?

Why not us? Here is a bunch of reasons why you should join us!

Support

We support our players financially, emotionally and physically by providing a range of services.

Open Schedule

Play when you want, no mandatory playtime when you are busy with college assignments. (We know how hectic it gets!)

Online Presence

Appear on camera and in live events (once everything goes back to normal) and livestream for the club!

Leave Whenever

Not your thing? Not enough time? Grounded? That's OK, you can leave at any time, no contract required!

Travel

Once COVID-19 is over, we will begin hosting tournaments away from home and abroad. Esports is a way to explore!

Enjoyment

You know that feeling when you win a something? Yeah, we do that a lot, that's who we are. It could be you too!
